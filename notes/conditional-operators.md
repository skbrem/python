## Table of common comparison operators used in Python

| Operator | Purpose | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `==` | Equal to | `a == b`
| `!=` | Not equal to | `a != b` |
| >= | Greater than or equal to | `a >= b`
| `>` | Greater than | `a > b` |
| `<` | Less than | `a < b` |
| <= | Less than or equal to | `a <= b` |
